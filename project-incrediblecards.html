Incredible Cards

Incredible Cards is an independent information provider about credit cards in Singapore and helps to choose the best card for you.

The client's request

Primary goal was to enable easy comparison of credit card products. Putting important characteristics of cards in the forefront and presenting them in understandable manner was the main challenge. Site also has many filtering options and enables visitors to easily leave comments.
